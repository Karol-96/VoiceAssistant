Ultra-Safe Design

7 SRS airbags - front, front side, full length curtain and driver's passenger side airbags. ISOFIX child restraint anchorage points (outer rear seats). Automatic emergency braking system with front and rear collision warning.

Power to Impress

AC and DC charging ports and 220V emergency charging cable. Approximately 45 minute charge time with Super Charger DC MAX (80kw).

Efficient & Intelligent

Lane departure warning, intelligent cruise control with ABS and traction control. Built-in Audio, phone, ACC and Multi-Function Display steering wheel controls.

Loads of Space

60/40 split fold rear seats with up to 1,330 litres of boot space with the rear seats down. 6-way driver and 4-way front passenger power adjusted seats.
